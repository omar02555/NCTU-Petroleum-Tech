About Us

Welcome to The 1st Student Chapter of NCTU Petroleum Tech

The 1st student chapter of NCTU Petroleum Tech at New Cairo Technological University (NCTU) that was founded in June, 2024 by students at the Production, Processing and Transport of Petroleum Department.
We are a non-profit organization that aims to enhance the students' skills, share knowledge among them, provide students with all information related to their career, and help them to meet their goals.

Our Mission

We are a non-profit professional page dedicated to developing and promoting technical knowledge in the field of oil and natural gas exploration and production, as well as enhancing communication among petroleum engineers worldwide and sharing knowledge.

Our Vision

NCTU PETROLUM TEC advances the oil and gas producing and related energy communities ability to meet the worlds energy needs in a safe, secure, and sustainable manner.

Our Mission

Mission

Educate & Train

Our goal is to educate and train the next generation of petroleum engineers by enhancing their technical knowledge, developing their soft skills, promoting research in the field, and serving the industry with professional expertise, ultimately striving to achieve an outstanding Student Chapter Award.

Mission

Introduce New Ideas

Our aim is to introduce fresh, innovative ideas and inspiring events that can transform our audiences perspectives and aspirations. This, in turn, will guide them in choosing fulfilling careers and ideal lifestyles.

Mission

Create Student Mentality

Our objective is to foster a student mindset dedicated to community service, equipping individuals to understand and address the challenges facing our society. .

Mission

Community of Thinkers

We aim to collaborate with partners and sponsors from other universities and organizations to build a community of thinkers who believe in the transformative power of ideasideas that can shape lives, alter perspectives, shift attitudes, and ultimately change the world.
